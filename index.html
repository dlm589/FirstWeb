Assignment 2 Web Page
Building Height In Comparison To Zoning Height

Building heights are regulated by the Toronto Zoning Bylaw. However, building developers can propose to build higher than the zoned height if they are willing to provide Section 37 community benefits to the city. In this map, you can view a 3D model of both the zoning height and the buildings in the city. Come and chekc out which buildings exceeded the zoned height!

Assignment 3 Web Map
Bike Station and Journey to Work

This map shows the possible relationship between biking station lovations and peoples' travel habits. If you take a look at the sustainable commute map, you will see that places with higher portion of people engaging in public transits, walking, and cycling as their main mode of commute will also have more bike stations. Enjoy the map!
